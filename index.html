About Me

I studied computer science in France, my native country, I got a bachelor and a master/engineer degrees in IT.

After graduating, I moved to Paris where I worked for 5 years in a service company for multiple big clients mostly with Java. I worked on high-traffic back-end applications and also websites with HTML/CSS.

Later, I wanted to be closer to clients and improve my expertise in development. I went to a smaller service company where I had the opportunity of developing a unique skill-set with Javascript, front-end frameworks and mobile apps.

Then I decided to take a big step and move to Vancouver, a city I visited a few years ago and I really liked. I am now working on client facing and E-commerce websites with Meteor and Grails.
